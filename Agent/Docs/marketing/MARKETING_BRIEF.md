## Executive Summary

Terlo is revolutionizing how local communities discover events and how organizers sell tickets. With platform fees up to 89% lower than Eventbrite ($0.99 vs $8.74 on a $100 ticket), Terlo enables event organizers to keep more of what they earn while providing attendees with a unified, map-based discovery platform for all local events.

**Current Status:**
- ✅ Fully launched and operational in Terre Haute, Indiana
- ✅ Complete ticketing platform with Stripe Connect integration
- ✅ Interactive map-based event discovery
- ✅ Business directory with 100+ listings
- ✅ Push notifications and real-time analytics
- ✅ Universal app (iOS, Android, Web)

**Expansion Goal:** Scale to additional mid-size cities (50K-250K population) across the Midwest and beyond.

## Messaging Do's and Don'ts

### DO:
✅ Lead with the fee savings (89% vs. Eventbrite)
✅ Use specific numbers ("Save $7.75 per ticket")
✅ Emphasize simplicity ("No account needed")
✅ Highlight community impact ("Keep money local")
✅ Show real examples and screenshots
✅ Be transparent about pricing
✅ Focus on benefits, not just features
✅ Use active, simple language
✅ Include clear calls-to-action

### DON'T:
❌ Use jargon or corporate speak
❌ Over-promise features not yet built
❌ Bash competitors by name (except fee comparisons)
❌ Assume technical knowledge
❌ Bury the pricing information
❌ Use vague claims ("save money" without numbers)
❌ Focus only on features without context
❌ Forget mobile-first mindset (most users are on phones)

## FAQs for Marketing Copy

### For Organizers

**Q: How much do you charge?**
A: Just $0.99 per ticket sold (for tickets $10+). For tickets under $10, we charge 3% with a $0.30 minimum. No monthly fees, no hidden costs. Compare that to Eventbrite's 6.95% + $1.79 per ticket.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Immediately! Funds are available in your Stripe account as soon as the transaction processes. No waiting weeks for platform payouts.

**Q: What if I need to refund a ticket?**
A: Easy. Process refunds directly through your dashboard with full Stripe integration. The platform fee is refunded automatically.

**Q: Do I need to provide my own payment processor?**
A: We handle everything through Stripe Connect. Just connect your bank account during setup—it takes about 5 minutes.

**Q: Can I still use Eventbrite for some events and Terlo for others?**
A: Absolutely! Many organizers start with one event on Terlo to test it out. Once they see the savings, they usually move everything over.

**Q: What kind of analytics do you provide?**
A: Full analytics dashboard with page views, unique visitors, conversion rates, ticket sales by type, and more. Plus UTM tracking for your marketing campaigns.

### For Attendees

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: Nope! You can buy tickets as a guest with just your email. Your tickets arrive instantly.

**Q: Where do my tickets go?**
A: Email, Apple Wallet, Google Pay—your choice. You'll also get a QR code for easy entry.

**Q: What if I lose my tickets?**
A: Just enter your email on our ticket recovery page. We'll send them again.

**Q: Are all local events on Terlo?**
A: We're working on it! We automatically aggregate events from multiple sources and partner with local organizers. The more people who use Terlo, the more complete our listings become.

**Q: Is it free to browse events?**
A: Completely free. No ads, no account required. Just open the app and explore.
